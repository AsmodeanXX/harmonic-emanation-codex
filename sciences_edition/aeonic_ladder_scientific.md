## 1. Introduction

The **Aeonic Ladder** is the hierarchical emergence model at the core of the Harmonic Emanation Codex (HEC).  
It describes how increasingly complex structures arise from the triaxial substrate:

- **Symplokē** — adjacency & relational structure  
- **Recurrence** — cyclic propagation & temporal ordering  
- **Ennoia** — coherence selection & tension minimization  

The Ladder is not a timeline, not a cosmological sequence, and not a metaphysical hierarchy.  
It is a **structural progression**, showing how:

1. relational primitives →  
2. structured patterns →  
3. stable cycles →  
4. dimensional geometry →  
5. matter →  
6. identity →  
7. consciousness →  
8. meaning →  
9. collective coherence →  
10. global integration  

…emerge through coherent layering.

This document provides a scientific summary of each Aeon.
