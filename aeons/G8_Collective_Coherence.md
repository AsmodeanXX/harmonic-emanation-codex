## The Role of Symplokē, Recurrence, and Ennoia

### **Symplokē**  
Connects minds to each other.  
(“Your model overlaps with mine.”)

### **Recurrence**  
Stabilizes communication over time.  
(“We keep understanding each other.”)

### **Ennoia**  
Selects stable shared meanings.  
(“This interpretation works for the group.”)

Together they produce:

- shared intentionality  
- cultural coherence  
- coordinated behavior  
- collective relevance  

This is the basis of society.
