# Ennoia  
### *The Principle of Coherence, Stability & Emergent Meaning*  
_By Joshua Crich_

---

## Overview

**Ennoia** is the third foundational principle of the Harmonic Emanation Codex.

If Symplokē is **connection**,  
and Recurrence is **continuity over time**,  
then Ennoia is:

> **The tendency of systems to settle into stable, coherent, meaningful patterns.**

It is the principle behind:

- order  
- stability  
- meaning  
- intention  
- self-organization  
- attractors  
- the reduction of internal tension  

Ennoia is what makes the universe *make sense*.

---

## What Ennoia Means

Ennoia is not a force or a mystical principle.  
It is the **universal drive toward lower tension and higher coherence**.

Systems governed by Ennoia:

- organize themselves  
- stabilize their patterns  
- reduce contradictions  
- minimize internal conflict  
- maintain consistency  
- adapt toward meaningful states  

Simply put:

> **Ennoia turns chaos into coherence.**
